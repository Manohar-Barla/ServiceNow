Navigating & Palette Indicator Meanings:

To select a question, click on the question number in the question palette on the right side of your screen. Note the following color status indicators:

1
Not Visited: You have not opened or viewed this question yet.
2
Visited but Not Answered: You have viewed the question, but have not selected any answer.
3
Answered: You have selected option(s) for the question.
4
Marked for Review: You have flagged this question for double checking later. It will show as purple.

Answering a Question:

  • For Single Choice questions, click the radio option. For Multiple Select questions, tick checkboxes.
  • To secure your answer and count it as Answered (Green), click the Save & Next button at the bottom of the Workspace.
  • Answers are cached securely in the browser and synchronized immediately with the system.
